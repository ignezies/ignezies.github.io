GoPlims

GoPlimsis a robust project management platform designed to streamline task assignments, tracking, and reporting within an organization. Focusing on role-based collaboration, it enables project managers to delegate tasks, monitor real-time progress, and generate insightful reports. Key features include an integrated Kanban dashboard for visual task organization, a reporting module for data-driven insights, and automated email notifications to keep everyone informed. GoPlims optimizes workflows, enhances accountability, and ensures efficient project tracking across all levels of management.

  • Role-Based Task Assignment
  • Kanban Dashboard
  • Real-Time Tracking
  • Comprehensive Reporting
  • Email Notifications
StethCare

StethCare is a comprehensive platform for lab test booking and order management, designed to streamline both customer interactions and internal operations. For customers, it provides an intuitive interface to browse, schedule, and book lab tests, ensuring a smooth and efficient user experience. Through flexible slot customization, customers can book tests at their convenience, with options for home collections or branch visits.

For StethCare administrators, the platform offers robust tools for managing orders, a built-in CRM for customer relations, and an order management system to track and process bookings efficiently. With a report delivery module, lab results can be shared promptly, improving turnaround times and customer satisfaction. This end-to-end solution empowers lab centers to deliver high-quality service while optimizing their internal workflows.

  • Lab Test Booking
  • Order Management
  • Integrated CRM
  • Report Delivery
  • Slot Customization

Optiristic

Optiristic is a versatile, location-based service delivery and booking platform designed for businesses and service providers to connect with local customers seamlessly. The app enables business owners to register, set up customizable service areas on Google Maps, and showcase their offerings to users in specific locations.

With Optiristic, users can browse and book services in their preferred area, ensuring convenience and accessibility. The platform empowers business owners with dynamic configuration options, including setting custom working hours, defining time intervals between booking slots, and modifying availability for specific days. Additionally, each business can personalize its sales page, making it a unique representation on the app.

  • Location-Based Services
  • Easy Service Discovery
  • Customizable Business Pages
  • Flexible Scheduling
  • Google Maps Integration
Appointments

Appointments is an innovative lab test booking application designed to provide users with a seamless and efficient way to schedule medical tests. The app allows users to select their city, choose between center visits or home collection, and then browse through a list of available lab tests specific to their location. After selecting the required tests, users complete a simple demographic form before being shown nearby testing centers.

The app ensures a smooth booking experience by displaying available time slots for chosen branches and offering secure payment processing through Razorpay. With its user-friendly interface and efficient workflow, Appointments streamlines the process of booking lab tests, making it easier for users to manage their health with minimal hassle.

  • Location-based Booking
  • Home Collection Option
  • Lab Test Selection
  • Demographic Form Collection
  • Nearby Branch Finder
  • Real-time Slot Availability
  • Secure Payment Gateway

TokenXpress

TokenXpress is an advanced queue management system designed to streamline the process of managing customer flow in service-based environments. The application allows users to seamlessly obtain tokens for various services and keeps them informed of their turn in the queue. It features a reception module that efficiently calls tokens, collects demographic information, and facilitates payment processing for services. Additionally, invoices are automatically generated upon successful payment.

The system also provides real-time room flow management, enabling users to view the order of lab tests and track room configurations in the branch. This ensures better organization and smoother service delivery, optimizing both customer and staff experiences. With TokenXpress, businesses can significantly improve operational efficiency, reduce wait times, and enhance customer satisfaction.

  • Queue Management
  • Token Disbursement
  • Demographics Collection
  • Payment Integration
  • Invoice Generation
  • Room Flow Management
  • Lab Test Ordering
  • Branch Configurations
Smart Report

Smart Report is a Node.js-based application tailored for automated medical report generation and patient health analytics. Designed to retrieve patient data directly from Aarthi Scans, the app calculates personalized health scores and generates insightful analytics using predefined medical ranges, providing an accurate overview of the patient's health status.

Using HTML templates to style the reports, Smart Report transforms the structured data into professionally designed PDF documents. Once generated, these PDFs are seamlessly uploaded to the client’s Reporting portal, allowing easy access and distribution. This application optimizes the reporting process, ensuring consistent quality and efficiency in medical report delivery.
